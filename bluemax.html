TECHNICAL DATA SHEET


BLUE MAX
Cleaner and Degreaser


Outstanding Characteristics:

BLUE MAX is an excellent all purpose cleaner and degreaser especially designed for industrial use. BLUE MAX is a versatile product for degreasing and cleaning machinery, equipment, auto and industrial engines, and vinyl and plastic. For all practical purposes, BLUE MAX is non-toxic to humans, aquatic life and animals. The major consistuents in this formulation are biodegradable.

Description:
BLUE MAX contains a balanced combination of detergents, water softeners, grease-dissolving chemicals, corrosion inhibitors and emulsifiers. This enables BLUE MAX to penetrate and dissolve build-ups of greases, waxes and soils. BLUE MAX is economical, safe to use on fabric and paints and non-corrosive to metals.
