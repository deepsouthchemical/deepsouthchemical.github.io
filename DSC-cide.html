TECHNICAL DATA SHEET


DSC CIDE
Bactericide


Description:
DSC CIDE is a highly effective, blended organic sulfur antimicrobial agent.

Application:
DSC Cide is effective in controlling all forms of bacteria, including the sulfate-reducing type which is particularly detrimental in fresh and brine-water drilling and packer fluids. Recommended treatment is 5 gallons per 100 barrels

Shipping and Handling:
DSC CIDE is packaged in 5 gallon pails. Opening on containers should always be kept closed when not in use. Protective clothing, including goggles and gloves is required. For skin contact, wash with large quantities of water. For eye contact, flush with large quantities of water and immediately seek medical attention.

DSC CIDE has a pungent odor and should therefore be used in a well ventilated area.
