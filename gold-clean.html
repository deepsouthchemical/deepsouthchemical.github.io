TECHNICAL DATA SHEET

DSC GOLD CLEAN

 

Outstanding Characteristics:

DSC GOLD CLEAN was developed to be the best of the surface cleaners. Advanced surfactant compounds in solvent based mud, DSC GOLD CLEAN quickly penetrate and dissolve the toughest synthetic drilling fluids, greases and oils.

Description:

DSC GOLD CLEAN is an advanced-formula surface cleaner developed by Deep South Chemical, Inc., manufactures of specialized wellbore displacement additives and product support for oilfield applications. Deep South Chemical, Inc. is committed to the development of high performance products which address health and safety and environmental concerns operators face each and every day. DSC GOLD CLEAN was developed by Deep South Chemical, Inc. staff chemists utilizing in-house laboratory facilities for extensive research and development testing.

Application: DSC GOLD CLEAN is effective when used in cold or warm, hard or soft water. DSC GOLD CLEAN is designed for use on all types of surfaces: painted, stainless steel and carbon steel.

* 1:2 to 1:5 for heavy synthetic drilling fluids, grease/oils and pipe dopes.

* 1:5 to 1:10 for moderate to heavy soils on hard surfaces.

* 1: 10 to 1:20 for moderate to light soils and general cleaning.

Dilute in hot or cold water, allow solution to penetrate for about one minute, agitate lightly then rinse.
