TECHNICAL DATA SHEET


DSC OS-50
Oxygen Scavenger


Description:
DSC OS-50 is a sulfite-based oxygen scavenger for fresh water and low density packer fluids. DSC OS-50 is utilized to reduce free oxygen in solids free packer fluids.


Application:
DSC OS-50 is used as an oxygen remover. It is more soluble in sea water and other brines than the powdered form. Recommended treatment is 5 Gallons per 100 barrels.


Packaging:
DSC OS-50 is packaged in 5 gallon pails.
