DSC Jetting Tools are built to clean tieback threads, seal faces, downhole casing, risers and B. O. P.'s.
These highly efficient jetting systems are used extensively worldwide as rental and sales items.
  • Purpose built to provide a large cleaning area
  • Available with either bullnose or box connection
  • Can be used in conjunction with brushing tools and downhole inspection camera systems
  • Nozzles are interchangeable
  • High flow rate through small nozzles
  • Effective high pressure cleaning for tieback areas
  • Reduces rig time
  • Cost effective
  • Prevents damage to wellhead thread seal surfaces or other sensitive areas
